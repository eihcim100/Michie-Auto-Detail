Privacy Policy - Michie Auto Detailing LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how Michie Auto Detailing LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you visit and use our website michieauto.com, our online forms (such as Jotform and Google Lead Forms), and our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you request our services, fill out forms, contact us, or interact with our website. This may include:

  • Contact Information: Your full name, address (for detailing location), phone number, and email address.
  • Vehicle Information: Details about your vehicle (make, model, year, type) relevant to the detailing service.
  • Payment Information: While we do not store full payment card details directly on our servers, we collect information necessary to process your payments via secure third-party payment processors.
  • Communication Data: Records of your communications with us, including phone calls to our answering service, text messages, and emails.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our mobile auto detailing services.
  • To schedule and confirm appointments.
  • To communicate with you about your service, appointments, and inquiries.
  • To process payments for services rendered.
  • To improve and personalize your experience with our services.
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Collect Information

We collect information through:

  • Online Forms: Data you submit via our Jotform portal or Google Lead Forms.
  • Phone Calls: Information provided during calls to our answering service.
  • Website Interaction: Basic technical data (like IP address, browser type) for website analytics, to understand how our site is used and improve it.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party companies that perform services on our behalf, such as our answering service, payment processors, and detailing contractors (to facilitate service delivery at your location). These providers are obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
